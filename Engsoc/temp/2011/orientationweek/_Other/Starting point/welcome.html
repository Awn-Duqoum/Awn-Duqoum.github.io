Hello and welcome to the official site of the University of Waterloo's Engineering Orientation Week! Here, you will find answers to all the questions you may have regarding the Engineering Faculty events of Orientation Week. You have chosen and been selected to attend the greatest Engineering University in Canada! Soon you will receive your invitation to Earn Your Hardhat, so check your mailbox for your welcome package soon. Earning your Hardhat is a longstanding Waterloo Engineering tradition that sets you apart from all of the other faculties at Waterloo. If you do not know what we're talking about, wait and see!

Each year there is a theme to our orientation week. The theme this year is 'Expedition: Earth' and each team represents an animal environment. During the week, you will be split into one of twelve teams to compete against each other in all of our events for ultimate orientation week victory! Each team is associated with a specific colour, and you'll be able to see which team has selected you later this summer. Also, don't forget to check out the rest of the website to learn more about our history and traditions, the schedule of the week, and to get some answers to the questions you may already have.

We, as your Engineering Federation Orientation Committee (EngFOC) hope that you are as thrilled as we are to start this amazing week. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us at foc@engmail.uwaterloo.ca. We promise that you will receive a personal reply from one of us!
