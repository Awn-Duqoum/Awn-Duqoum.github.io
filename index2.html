My Experiences

IJS - Intellijoint Surgical Inc.

The coop term I spent at IJS was unlike any of my previous coop experiences. The term challenged to learn a tremendous about human anatomy, image analysis, metrology, and machine learning. I had the fortunate experience to work on a large variety of projects, the largest being my work with Active Shape Models (ASM) to extract key landmarks from X-Ray images to help orthopedic surgeons with preoperative templating and postoperative validation. I was also able to utilize Backpropagation Networks to develop a gesture recognition kernel that allowed surgeons using the Intellijoint Hip product to interact with the system wirelessly.

NDI - Northern Digital

The two CO-OP terms I spent with NDI were the most educational and exciting terms of my university career. I had the fortunate experience of creating a multitude of tools that would aid the research and development process for years to come. Among those tools was a Bamboo build server to automate the testing process of new code releases. Another tool that was developed was the “Production Data Visualization Toolkit” which helped transform the data collection and visualization process from a tedious undertaking to a trivial effort. While at NDI I got to learn a lot about image processing, data analysis, signal processing and system design.
